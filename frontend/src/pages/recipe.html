Delicious Chocolate Cake

A rich and moist chocolate cake that’s perfect for celebrations.

Dessert Vegetarian Under 1 Hour

Recipe by Chef Marie

Prep Time: 20 mins
Cook Time: 30 mins
Total Time: 50 mins
Servings: 8
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x9 inch pan.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add oil, milk, eggs, and vanilla. Mix until smooth.
  4. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. Let the cake cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack.
